BIRKENSTOCK IS

a sustainable brand of footwear that contours to the owner's unique footprint.
We want to give everyone comfortable, sustainable and stylish footwear, but some people don’t think Birkenstocks are for them — the shoes seem too crunchy, too edgy, or too ugly.

The Task

Spread brand awareness across diverse demographics by focusing on Birkenstock’s timeless style and functionality.



The Job

Spread brand awareness

Represent fashion and function

Be authentic

We identified the following personas that
best fit the Birkenstock brand.


People from all demographics can benefit from wearing Birkenstocks. We’re talking specifically to the digitally-active who can benefit from Birkenstock footwear’s unique sole in their everyday life.

Execution

Same Sole

All Birkenstock shoes have the same base sole, made of the same latex and cork.
In this way, Birkenstocks are a great unifier — they preserve individuality, but also keep everyone stepping on common ground.

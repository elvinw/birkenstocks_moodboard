One Sole for All

Birkenstocks is a brand of footwear designed with sustainable material that contours to the owner's unique footprint. We determined the primary problem in this brand ecosystem is that consumers do not think that Birkenstocks are for them.

The Job

1. Spread brand awareness across varying demographics
2. Sell customers a versatile shoe that's both fashionable and functional
3. Show that Birkenstock footwear embodies the best of both worlds through its style and comfort.
